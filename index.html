Common Issues

Problems We Solve

Our technicians are trained to diagnose and resolve a wide range of printer issues — fast.

Printer Not Printing

Printer is powered on but no output. We diagnose spooler issues, queue errors, and driver conflicts.

Paper Jam Issues

Repeated paper jams, false jam errors, or paper feeding problems. We clear and recalibrate the feed mechanism.

Printer Offline Error

Printer showing offline status on Windows or Mac. We restore connection and fix TCP/IP port settings.

Print Quality Issues

Faded prints, streaks, lines, or incorrect colors. We clean print heads and recalibrate color profiles.

Wireless Not Connecting

Printer won't connect to Wi-Fi or drops connection. We configure network settings and fix IP conflicts.

Driver Not Found

Driver missing, corrupted, or incompatible with OS. We install certified drivers for all OS versions.

Ink/Toner Not Recognized

Cartridge error or toner chip issues. We resolve cartridge detection problems and update firmware.

Printer Spooler Error

Print jobs stuck or spooler keeps stopping. We repair Windows spooler service and clear corrupt jobs.

Slow Printing

Prints taking too long. We optimize printer settings, update drivers, and fix network bottlenecks.

Error Codes

E-series, B-series, or numeric error codes. We decode and resolve all manufacturer error messages.

Scanner Not Working

Scanner undetected or producing blank scans. We reconfigure TWAIN/WIA drivers and scan software.

Mobile Print Failure

AirPrint, Google Cloud Print, or HP ePrint issues. We configure cloud and mobile printing end-to-end.

Firmware Update Issues

Failed firmware updates or printer stuck in update loop. We safely recover and update your device.

Shared Network Printing

Printer not visible on home or office network. We configure shared printing across all devices.

Printer Won't Turn On

Power button unresponsive or printer shuts off immediately. We diagnose hardware and power issues.

Duplex / Two-Sided Failure

Automatic duplex printing not working. We configure duplexer settings and fix mechanical faults.
